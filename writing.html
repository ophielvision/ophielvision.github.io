The Receiver Operates Automatically

The receiver operates automatically.
The current flows only while motion is taking place.
When a new queen takes control,
the old queen and thousands of workers
desert the hive to establish a new home.

The eye is like a camera.
It is first filtered through rocks.

The lens throws an inverted image.
The entire cast assembled.
With railroads radiating in every direction,
The scissors kick.
See her body swell as she sucks the blood of her victim.

This is What Happened to David's Bones

This is what happened to David’s bones.
With eyes opened through study,
Jim sees a way to improve a machine and simplify a process.
How to restore respiration when breathing has ceased.

This is the kind of place where the fly will lay her eggs.
The dark ‘spots’ we sometimes see are holes.
This one is big enough to hold the Earth.

When the direction of the current is reversed,
the direction of the lines of force is also reversed.
At twelve days, the life blood begins to circulate.
Boy scouts from another land show us some new stunts.

This is How we Swim Through Water

Hatching through the egg case.
Learning body balance; submerge face,
float relaxed. The air's weight
makes possible the parachute.

This is how we swim through water,
the dark place
in the center of the crater.
The swan dive. The electric torch a diver
uses. The star reporter
on special assignment.
In some such way a bird flies.
Note how they rise
and how they light.
Every year the group grows smaller.

A sending station. A kite
floats in the air by being pulled
against the wind, testing the eyes
To see better what the Moon looks like,
To choose for every need and appetite.

What do you see
out of your window at night?
A rudder to steer to left
or right.
